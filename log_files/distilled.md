### Reading Roadmap (from `quant_map.txt`)

**Beginner**
- *Quantitative Trading* — Ernest P. Chan (systematic trading, backtesting, strategy design)
- *Hands-On AI for Trading* — Ernest P. Chan (ML + trading in practice)

**Intermediate**
- *Measure, Integration and Real Analysis* — Sheldon Axler (measure theory foundation)
- *Variational Calculus and Optimal Control* — John L. Troutman (optimization intuition → portfolio optimization)
- *Numerical Optimization* — Nocedal & Wright (gradient descent, Newton, quasi-Newton, constrained optimization)
- *Markov Chain Monte Carlo* — Gamerman & Lopes (probabilistic inference, Bayesian finance)

**Advanced Core**
- *Stochastic Differential Equations* — Bernt Øksendal (Itô integrals, SDEs, Black–Scholes foundation)
- *Stochastic Calculus for Finance I* — Steven Shreve (binomial trees, arbitrage pricing)
- *Stochastic Calculus for Finance II* — Steven Shreve (continuous-time, martingales, Itô, Black–Scholes in depth)

**Also from notes (`quant resources.txt`, `more quant.txt`)**
- *An Introduction to Quantitative Finance* — Stephen Blyth
- *A First Course in Quantitative Finance* — Thomas Mazzoni
- *Systematic Trading* — Robert Carver
- *Options, Futures and Other Derivatives* — Hull
- *Option Volatility and Pricing* — Sheldon Natenberg
- *Bayesian Statistics the Fun Way*
- *Thinking Strategically*
- *The Razor's Edge* (mentioned)
- Ross Probability (referenced without full title)
